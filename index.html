Brian's Job History

Teacher

I stumbled into a job as a high school Latin teacher. It turned out to be fun.

After a few years I realized I wasn't learning as much as I had at the beginning. I got restless and decided I needed a Ph.D. in Philosophy. It was obvious.

I spent three years at the University of Oxford doing a post-doc. This spoiled me, among other things. When that was over, a job in philosophy no longer seemed so important. What other job was going to compare to Oxford? I returned to teaching Latin.

I found more to learn in the new Latin job. But the learning has again declined over time, and I miss having a challenge. I've been studying basic web development technologies for the past couple of years, and I hope to change careers in 2022.
